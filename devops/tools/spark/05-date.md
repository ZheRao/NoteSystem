# Date & Timestamp Functions

## 1. Common Functions

**Now**
- `F.current_date`, `F.current_timestamp`

**Extract**
- `F.year`, `F.month`, `F.dayofmonth`, `F.dayofweek`, `F.weekofyear`, `F.quarter`
- `F.date_format(ts, fmt)`

**Parse/convert**
- `F.to_date`, `F.to_timestamp`
- `F.unix_timestamp`, `F.from_unixtime`

**Diff**
- `F.datediff`
- `F.months_between`
